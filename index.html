As you can see, each chunk is taxed at a progressively higher rate.

Your income is not uniformly taxed at the highest rate!

MYTH:

"If I work another job it might bump me into a new tax bracket and cause me to loose money."

FACT:

Only the "new" money that you earned at your second job will be taxed at the "new" rate.

Math time!

Let's calculate exactly how much you owe

  • 10% of $9,225 is $922.50
  • 15% of $28,225 is $4,233.75
  • 25% of $12,550 is $3,137.50

So, you would owe the federal government $8,293.75 before deductions.
